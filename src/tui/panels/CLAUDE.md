- **`_gallery` lazy-init, never refreshed mid-session** (`overlays.ts`): `createBadgeGallery` runs once when `_gallery` is null. Achievements earned while overlay is open won't appear. `closeBadgeGallery()` is the only path that resets `_page`, `_gallery`, and `_badgeScroll` together — call it on overlay close, not just on explicit badge-page exit.
- **`borderColor` computed twice in `renderDetailRows`** (`detail.ts`): inside the switch (cache-miss path) and again after the cache block (authoritative). The post-cache recomputation overwrites the switch assignments — add custom border logic to the post-cache block, not the switch, or cache hits always use `'gray'`.
